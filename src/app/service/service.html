Hair Care Tips

Daily Maintenance

  • Use sulfate-free shampoos to preserve color
  • Apply heat protectant before styling
  • Sleep on silk pillowcases to reduce friction
  • Regular trims every 6-8 weeks for healthy growth

Seasonal Care

  • Summer: UV protection and hydrating treatments
  • Winter: Moisture-rich products and scalp care
  • Spring/Fall: Perfect time for color changes and new styles
